Our Restaurant

We serve organic snails in our restaurant.

Why?

Snails are well-known as protein rich mollusks and is similar to the protein found in pork and beef, but snails come with a much lower fat content. In addition to containing significant sources of protein and low amounts of fat, snails are also good sources of iron, calcium, Vitamin A, and a number of other minerals.
